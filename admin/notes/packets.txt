Nude MVC: A basic MVC boilerplate. You have to add all the other functionality yourself.

Dressed MVC: A basic MVC with a provided admin panel to create and manage users, rights, contact(client) information.
a front- and back-end integration.
Basic file-upload solution.
Templates can also be made for the  admin panel to meet your customers needs.
User/contact information is encrypted with AES 128 algorithm. 

Suit MVC: All functionality included in Dressed MVC plus: Storing encrypted texts. Send encrypted messages to
other users.

Dress to Impress MVC: Webshop integration.
